Pycraft

The game of Minecraft has been a huge success in education and the world of casual gamers. Millions of people have bought the game and enjoy playing hours, coming up with innovative concepts and designs. I myself am a fan of Minecraft and I applaud the people who make a living out of making gameplay videos on YouTube.

Recently I was looking to expand my Python skills and decided to create a Minecraft-like game in the terminal. This project was a collaboration with olls. The game is quite advanced so far, with the following features:

  • Random terrain generation with ores, hills and trees
  • Auto-saving world and multiple save support
  • Chunk saving to improve performance
  • Breaking and placing blocks
  • Inventory management
  • Custom world options

We plan on taking the project further, with things like crafting and other block types.
